## Implementation Notes

- **Blog route:** Create `/blog` in the Next.js app with an index page listing posts and individual post pages at `/blog/[slug]`.
- **Author schema:** Every post needs `author: Neil Barris` with a link to `/about` and a visible publish date.
- **Internal linking:** Each post should link to at least 2 other pages (mix of blog and landing pages).
- **Publish cadence:** 1 post per week is sustainable. Don't publish 10 posts at once — staggered publishing looks more natural to Google.
- **Word count:** Aim for 1,200–1,500 words minimum. Post 10 (Windsor Parke extended) should be 2,000+.
